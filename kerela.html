CULTURE OF KERELA

Kerala's culture is a vibrant, ancient blend of Dravidian and Aryan traditions, shaped by diverse influences to create a unique Malayali identity. Known as "God's Own Country," it features rich performing arts like Kathakali and Mohiniyattam, the martial art Kalaripayattu, traditional Ayurveda, and grand festivals like Onam. The culture is deeply rooted in religious harmony, with a strong emphasis on literature, coconut-based cuisine, and, for many, the backwaters.
